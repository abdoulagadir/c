Agadir to small Desert


 

40 €
Booking

 1 day trip


 

Discover the region Massa and Visit her small desert village is located 55 km south of Agadir. We invite you to experience the spirit of  desert , its silence, magic dunes, sunsets like nowhere else. and you discover the inhabitants and their traditions with ease in a real shared happiness.


Departure from the hotel by 4x4 to the south of Agadir to discover wild beaches uncrowded a sandbank end of the Atlantic coast and visit the village of seamen.

visiting a real natural park where wildlife refuge and is still wild place where flora Ibis bats and other birds. A sandy track runs along the Oued Massa until the beach of Sidi R'bat.


visit Dam Youssef bin Tachfine of the Oued Massa.
there bar one of the arms of the Oued Massa, forming an artificial lake of almost 7 km long


Discover the small desert oasis and its banana plantations. A free time for taking photos ... Then discover the berber village where you will be in a special appointment to take a traditional lunch (included) at the inhabitant, before continuing on to Tiznit to make a visit of the ramparts of this city extraordinary that became famous for the manufacture of jewelry in silver.
