Enjoy a jet-ski experience in Agadir Beach.

Thrilling one-hour ride over the waves

A different perspective on the Agadir coastline.

this jet-ski ride along the Agadir beach is a must.

After a quick briefing at base, the fun begins. Zooming out to sea, you'll leap through the waves as you enjoy views of the bright blue water and white sand beaches. And as most jet-ski are built for two, you can choose between taking control in the driver's seat and admiring the scenery as a passenger.

Then, after some time at sea, you'll do the trip in reverse. Picking up speed on the return, you'll power through the water, feeling the wind in your hair. All in all, it's one adrenaline-pumping experience.
